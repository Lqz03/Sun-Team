Led by Dr. Sun Daner, our research team at The Education University of Hong Kong explores the transformative potential of emerging technologies in education. We focus on AI-powered learning systems, technology-enhanced STEM education, and innovative pedagogical approaches that foster higher-order thinking across disciplines. Through rigorous research and meaningful collaboration with schools and communities, we strive to create equitable, evidence-based educational solutions that prepare learners for an AI-driven future.
