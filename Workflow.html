Dr. Sun Daner's Research Team Hosts Workshop with Professor Alexandra Cristea from Durham University


On November 3, 2025, Dr. Sun Daner's Research Team at The Education University of Hong Kong hosted a collaborative workshop with Professor Alexandra Cristea from Durham University. During the workshop, Dr. Sun Daner and her team presented their ongoing research projects and innovative work in AI-enhanced education, technology-integrated STEM learning, and adaptive learning platforms.

Professor Cristea, a distinguished expert in adaptive educational systems and technology-enhanced learning, engaged in insightful discussions with the team about potential synergies and future collaboration opportunities. The exchange of ideas covered topics including personalized learning technologies, intelligent tutoring systems, and user modeling in educational contexts.

This workshop represents a valuable opportunity for international academic exchange, fostering connections between leading researchers in educational technology and paving the way for future collaborative research initiatives.
